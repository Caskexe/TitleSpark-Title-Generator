# TitleSpark - Title / Working Title Generator

Stuck on a title? Let TitleSpark ignite your next hit - or your next big idea!

***TitleSpark*** creates one or several titles with a single click. Generate fresh, unexpected song titles or working names for any creative project. Slide the mood from dark and moody to bright and hopeful, and watch inspiration flow.
The word list is comprehensive and covers many themes (a future update will expand genres and categories) - but unlike most online generators, the titles generated make sense and don't add a multitude of junk words.

Whether you’re composing music, writing, or brainstorming, TitleSpark helps you:
Discover unique song titles, working titles, or placeholder names
Explore moods from moody and mysterious to uplifting and hopeful
Quickly copy ideas to keep your creative sessions moving

Perfect for albums, EPs, or any project that needs a spark, TitleSpark turns a blank page into endless possibilities.

- or download the html file and run locally.
